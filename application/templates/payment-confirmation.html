What happens next

Ofsted staff will:

  • review your application
  • ask for references
  • carry out checks with social services
  • check the health booklet
  • arrange for an inspector to visit

All this normally takes 3 months in total.

We will email you after the initial review.

There can be delays if information is missing or we have to wait for references or social services checks.

We will organise a visit once all the checks have been done.

You should get a decision within a week of the visit.
