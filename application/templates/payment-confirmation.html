What happens next

Ofsted staff will:

  • review your application
  • ask for references
  • carry out checks with social services
  • check the health booklet
  • arrange for an inspector to visit

All this usually takes about 3 months in total.

There can be delays if information is missing or we have to wait for references or social services checks.

Once all the checks have been done, an inspector will call you to arrange a date when they can visit.

The inspector will tell you their recommendation on the day of the visit.
