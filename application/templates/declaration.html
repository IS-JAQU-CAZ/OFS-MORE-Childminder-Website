Ofsted will have to check your details, inspect your home and interview you after you apply.

The interview will include questions on the Early Years Foundation Stage (EYFS).

What is the EYFS?

The EYFS contains the standards for caring for young children.

An inspector will ask how you will keep children safe and help them learn.

You should go on a training course if you don't understand the EYFS.

EYFS courses cost around £200. Ask your council for details.
