Costs of becoming a childminder

Register as a childminder
Application fee (not refundable) £35

The fee is £103 if you are only caring for children aged over 5.

Criminal record (DBS) checks
Your criminal record (DBS) check £52.10
Checks for adults in your home £52.10 each
Health declaration booklet
GP to fill in and sign booklet £90 (approx)
Training
Paediatric first aid course £80 to £200 (approx)
Early years training £20 to £200 (approx)
Insurance
Public liability insurance £25 to £100 (approx)
Digital personal details records
Keep digital records of children - Register with ICO £35

Your house and car insurance also need to be up to date.
