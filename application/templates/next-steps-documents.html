Documents you need for the visit

The inspector will need to check your identity and other documents when they visit your home.

Document type Examples
Photo ID Passport or driving licence
Proof of address (2 pieces of evidence) Gas, electricity or water bill
Credit card, bank or mortgage statement
Proof of any name changes Marriage certificate or decree absolute
Employment history CV or details of where you have worked
First aid Paediatric first aid certificate
Childcare qualifications Certificates (if you have any)
Criminal record (DBS) checks Certificates for everyone aged 16 or older
Insurance Personal liability insurance
Driving documents Driving licence, car insurance and MOT certificate (if driving children around)
