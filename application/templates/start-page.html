Apply to be a childminder (England)

Apply to be a childminder to look after children in your home.

Before you start

You will have to get criminal record checks for everyone aged 16 or over in your home.

Criminal record checks are also known as 'DBS checks'.

We will need details of:

  • your criminal record check
  • household members' checks
  • your first aid training
  • 2 references
  • your address history
  • any health problems

You will need to have a good understanding of the Early Years Foundation Stage if you are caring for children under 5.

How long it takes

It can take about 6 months to register with Ofsted.

It should take you 30 minutes to fill in this form.

How much it costs

It can cost a few hundred pounds to become a childminder.

It usually costs £35 per year to register with Ofsted.

You will also have to pay for things like criminal record checks and training courses.
