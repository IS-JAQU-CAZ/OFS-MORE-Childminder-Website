Register as a childminder (England)

Register as a childminder to look after children in your home.

Before you start

You will need the following:

  • criminal record (DBS) certificate
  • DBS certificates for anyone over 16 in your home
  • paediatric first aid certificate
  • health declaration booklet
  • 2 references

Childcare training

You will have to show how you will follow the Early Years Foundation Stage standards for children under 5.

You should go on a training course if you don't know how you will meet the standards.

Contact your council for details of courses in your area.

How much it costs

It usually costs £35 to register with Ofsted.

You will have to pay for your own DBS and health checks, training and equipment.

Find out more about costs.

How long it takes

It should take you 30 minutes to fill in this form.

It takes about 3 months to register with Ofsted.

An inspector will visit your home if you will be looking after children aged 5 or under.
